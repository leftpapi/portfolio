How to play

Guess the mystery BedWars kit. After each guess, every column lights up:

  • GREEN — exact match
  • YELLOW — close (length within 2)
  • GRAY — no match

Length and Words show an arrow (↑/↓) telling you if the answer's value is higher or lower than your guess.

You get 5 guesses per round. Daily mode gives everyone the same kit each day, resetting at midnight. Unlimited mode is a random kit every time — play as much as you want.
